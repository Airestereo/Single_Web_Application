About Us

Airstereo is a global, multi-platform media and entertainment company. Powered by its own proprietary technology, Airstereo is the go-to source for tech, digital culture and entertainment content for its dedicated and influential audience around the globe.

Why Choose Us?

Airstereo marries art and science to benefit your brand. Our Velocity technology suite is a patented creative services platform, powered by data science and real-time machine learning. Velocity is used throughout the creative process to predict to create and to target.

PREDICT

Using VelocityDash, our content creators eliminate the guesswork. We use data - not our gut - to discover what content your target audience loves.

OPTIMIZE

Exclusive Velocity insights are then used to inform and inspire our creativity, delivering you custom branded concepts based on exclusive data.

TARGET

Once live, VelocityKg optimizes your content in real time, driving organic shares and targeting real influencers, not social media celebrities. We put your content in front of the right people.
